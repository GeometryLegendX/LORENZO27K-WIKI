Introduction

Lorenzo27K [1] is a Geometry Dash YouTuber known for his insane skill and consistent streaming. He often streams a few times a week and frequently plays extreme demons. Sometimes he also plays other games like Five Nights at Freddy’s [2] after huge demand from his viewers.

Completions

Lorenzo27K reached his first big milestone when he beat Nine Circles by ZoBros [3]. After about 2,000 attempts with a highest heart rate of 149 BPM, he beat it about four months after Future Funk, and the grind took him around one month. He completed it on the 19th of January 2025.

Lorenzo27K then went for Future Funk by JonathanGD [4]. After over 3,000 attempts and a highest heart rate of 201 BPM, it took him about three weeks. He completed it on the 23rd of February 2025.

After that, he made a huge jump to Bloodbath by Riot and more [5]. It took him over 42,000 attempts, two mouse smashes, one headphone smash, and one mouse pad that was thrown off his desk. After more than 70 hours of playtime, he finally beat it on the 18th of January 2026.

After that achievement, he verified Stereo Madness.

He later created his own level called The Mimbo [6], a memory demon inspired by LIMBO. It took him over 18,000 attempts with a top heart rate of 157 BPM. The level was verified live on stream on the 15th of February 2026.

He also beat several so-called "poopy levels", which are layout versions of popular levels that are slightly nerfed. His best completion was Poopy Shardscapes [7], which took him around 3,000 attempts with a top heart rate of 161 BPM.
