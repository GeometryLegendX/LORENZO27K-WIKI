Overview

Lorenzo27K is a content creator known for playing Geometry Dash, a rhythm platformer with user-generated levels that can sometimes take insane skill to beat. He streams and uploads gameplay of challenging levels, especially Demon-rated levels, which fall under the hardest of all levels in the game. He also sometimes plays other things like Five Nights at Freddy's if viewers request it. He started his chanel december 30th 2021. He has about 1.6 million views in total views in total

Notable Completions

Future Funk

Completed by Lorenzo27K, after 3,000+ attempts. Bright visuals, complex dual sections. Created by JonathanGD, he beat Future Funk. This took him about 2 weeks, and it was his first hard demon. He reached a insane heart rate of over 201 BPM, which is almost 50 BPM faster than the heart rate of the first person to walk on the moon, which is actually insane. But his work paid off, because on February 2025, he uploaded his completion video for the world to see.

Nine Circles

Completed after roughly 2,000 attempts. Level created by Zobros. He reached a top heart rate of 149 BPM after beating the level, even tho his heart rate was pretty consistent through out the rest of the run. It took him an intire month of constant grinding, but afer all that hard work, he beat the level with a insane reaction and published his completion video january 19th 2025.

Bloodbath

Extreme Demon verified by Riot. Took over 42,000 attempts and 70+ hours of playtime. He reached a top heart rate of 173 BPM. While beating this, some unfortunate moments occurred, being: 2 mouse smashes, 2 thrown headphones, and a poor mouspad that was thrown on the floor with the speed of a rocket engine, and probably minor damages to his voice after all the screaming. Practicing this level also took a fysical and mental tol on him, he had breakdowns, and he wanted to drop the level really badly, he almost did not touch grass. But on January 18th 2026, he beat Bloodbath and showed his completion to the world

The Mimbo

Original level by Lorenzo27K, memory-based Demon inspired by LIMBO. Completed after 18,000+ attempts. He built this level my himself and while beating it he reached a top heart rate of 157 BPM, He verified it live on stream on Ferbruary 15th 2026. Around this time was also when he got consistent viewers on his streams.

shitty Shardscapes

One of the joke “shitty levels”, completed after ~3,000 attempts. "Shitty shardscapes" was one of many "shitty" levels that he beat, but this one was his hardest. He beat it February 18th 2026. He almost quit the level, but in his last few attempts, he actually beat it with a top hart rate of 161 BPM. (if you watch this, maybe a good idea to turn your volume down when watching this, i actually got a ringing sound in my ear after hearing that)
