Lorenzo27K

Overview

Lorenzo27K is a gaming content creator primarily known for playing the rhythm platformer game Geometry Dash. The game is known for its extremely difficult levels and fast-paced gameplay that requires precise timing and strong muscle memory. Players often spend thousands of attempts learning and completing levels.

Lorenzo27K is known for streaming and uploading gameplay of challenging levels, particularly Demon-rated levels. His content usually focuses on completing difficult levels, practicing challenging sections, and sharing his progress with viewers during livestreams.

Occasionally, he also plays other games when requested by viewers, including horror games such as Five Nights at Freddy’s.

Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer game developed by RobTop Games. Players control a cube (or other forms such as ships, waves, and robots) that automatically moves forward through a level while the player must jump or fly to avoid obstacles. The game is widely known for its user-generated levels, many of which reach extremely high levels of difficulty.

The hardest levels in the game are usually classified as Extreme Demons. These levels often require thousands or even tens of thousands of attempts before completion.

Notable Completions

Nine Circles

One of Lorenzo27K’s early milestone completions was Nine Circles, a Hard Demon level created by Zobros. The level is famous for its distinctive wave gameplay and flashy visual effects, which later inspired a large number of similar levels in the community.

According to his stream statistics, Lorenzo27K completed the level after approximately 2,000 attempts. During the final completion his heart rate reportedly reached 149 BPM.

Future Funk

Another major completion was Future Funk, a long and complex Demon level created by JonathanGD. The level is widely known for its bright visuals, synchronized music gameplay, and difficult dual sections near the end of the level.

Lorenzo27K reportedly completed the level after more than 3,000 attempts, with a recorded peak heart rate of 201 BPM during the final moments of the level.

Bloodbath

One of Lorenzo27K’s most significant achievements was completing Bloodbath, an Extreme Demon originally verified by the Geometry Dash player Riot. Bloodbath is considered one of the most historically important levels in the game and was once ranked among the hardest levels ever created.

The level required over 42,000 attempts and more than 70 hours of playtime. During the long progression process, Lorenzo27K humorously mentioned breaking multiple pieces of equipment, including two computer mice and a pair of headphones.

He eventually completed Bloodbath on January 18, 2026.

The Mimbo

After completing several major levels, Lorenzo27K also created his own level titled The Mimbo. The level is a memory-based Demon inspired by the extremely difficult level LIMBO.

Memory levels require players to memorize invisible triggers, fake paths, and hidden mechanics rather than relying only on visual reaction time.

The level reportedly took more than 18,000 attempts to verify and was completed live on stream on February 15, 2026.

Poopy Shardscapes

Lorenzo27K has also completed several joke or challenge levels, sometimes referred to as “poopy levels” within the community. One example is Poopy Shardscapes, which took around 3,000 attempts to complete.
