Overview

Lorenzo27K is a content creator known for playing Geometry Dash, a rhythm platformer with user-generated levels. He streams and uploads gameplay of challenging levels, especially Demon-rated levels.

Notable Completions

Nine Circles

Completed by Lorenzo27K after roughly 2,000 attempts. Level created by Zobros.

Future Funk

Completed after 3,000+ attempts. Bright visuals, complex dual sections. Created by JonathanGD.

Bloodbath

Extreme Demon verified by Riot. Took over 42,000 attempts and 70+ hours of playtime.

The Mimbo

Original level by Lorenzo27K, memory-based Demon inspired by LIMBO. Completed after 18,000+ attempts.

Poopy Shardscapes

One of the joke “poopy levels”, completed after ~3,000 attempts.

Streaming

Lorenzo27K streams live attempts, interacts with viewers, and practices difficult levels in real time.
