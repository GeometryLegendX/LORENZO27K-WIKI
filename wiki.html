Introduction

Lorenzo27K [1] is a Geometry Dash YouTuber known for his insane skill and consistent streaming. He often streams several times a week and commonly plays extreme demons. He sometimes also plays other games like Five Nights at Freddy’s [2] after heavy request from his viewers.

Completions

His first big milestone was beating Nine Circles by ZoBros [3]. With around 2,000 attempts and a peak heart rate of 149 BPM, he finally beat it on January 19th, 2025.

Next he took on Future Funk by JonathanGD [4]. Over 3,000 attempts later, with a max heart rate of 201 BPM, he beat it on February 23rd, 2025.

He then made a massive jump to Bloodbath by Riot & more [5]. This one was brutal: 42,000+ attempts, two mouse smashes, one headphone smash, and one mousepad throw. Total grind time was over 70 hours, completed January 18th, 2026.

After that, he “verified” Stereo Madness (classic lol).

Later he created his own level: The Mimbo [6], a memory demon inspired by LIMBO. 18,000+ attempts, top HR 157 BPM, verified on stream February 15th, 2026.

He also completed several so-called “poopy levels.” His best one was Poopy Shardscapes [7], taking around 3,000 attempts and a peak HR of 161 BPM.
