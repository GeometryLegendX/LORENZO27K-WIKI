Lorenzo27K Wiki

Introduction

Lorenzo27K [1] is a Geometry Dash YouTuber known for his extreme skill and consistent streaming. He often streams multiple times a week and frequently plays extreme demons. Sometimes he also plays other games like Five Nights at Freddy’s [2] after viewer demand.

Completions

Lorenzo27K reached his first big milestone when he beat Nine Circles by ZoBros [3]. After about 2,000 attempts with a highest heart rate of 149 BPM, he beat it on January 19th, 2025.

He later completed Future Funk by JonathanGD [4]. After more than 3,000 attempts and a highest heart rate of 201 BPM, he beat it on February 23rd, 2025.

After that he made a huge jump to Bloodbath by Riot and more [5]. It took him over 42,000 attempts, two mouse smashes, one headphone smash, and one thrown mousepad. He finally completed it on January 18th, 2026 after more than 70 hours of playtime.

After this achievement he jokingly "verified" Stereo Madness.

Later he created his own level called The Mimbo [6], a memory demon inspired by LIMBO. It took him over 18,000 attempts with a top heart rate of 157 BPM. The level was verified live on stream on February 15th, 2026.

He also beat several so-called "poopy levels". His best completion was Poopy Shardscapes [7], which took around 3,000 attempts with a top heart rate of 161 BPM.
